Our Story

Founded in 2022 by Jahid Ansari, NexGenAiTech emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between cutting-edge artificial intelligence and practical business applications.

What started as a passion project has evolved into a full-service AI and Full-Stack Development company, serving clients across India and globally.

We believe that technology should be an enabler, not a barrier. Our approach combines technical expertise with business understanding to deliver solutions that drive real results.
